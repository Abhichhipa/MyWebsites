Empowering Students Through Meaningful Work

About SUPW

SUPW (Socially Useful Productive Work) is an integral part of the educational curriculum aimed at instilling practical skills and social responsibility among students. It enables students to work on projects that benefit their communities while gaining hands-on experience.

Projects

  • Environmental Conservation: Tree planting and waste management.
  • Community Service: Organizing health camps and literacy programs.
  • Craft and Artistry: Handmade products and creative arts.
